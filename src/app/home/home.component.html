Fender Jaguar Donny Barr

This is Donny's signature model created in collaboration with Fender and released in 2017. The pickups are deliberately wired out of phase for that distinctive Donny Barr sound.

Gibson Les Paul Bo Jonabassa

Made in 1957 in Gibson's factory in Kalamazoo, Michigan, this is one of the finest guitars in Bo's huge collection of vintage gear.

Fender Stratocaster Dravid Krilmour

Dravid's famous "black strat" which fetched a staggering $3,950,000 for charity when it was sold at auction by Christies in 2019. Played on famous albums "Dark Slide of the Balloon" and "The Crawl".
